Executive Summary



This application is developed to keep track of employeesâ€™ profile, scheduling and leaves. There are two sections employee and administrator. Administrator has access to various sections like schedule administration, company administration, employee administration, message center and report section. Employee schedule is equipped with day wise and template scheduling. Drag and drop functionality make it easier for the users to manage scheduling. Employee can view his/her own schedules on the calendar. Employee can apply for leave on the calendar using his/her login and send request to another employee to reallocate his own schedule with him. Employee can also view his/her own profile and update it.
